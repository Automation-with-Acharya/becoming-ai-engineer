# MENTOR_RULES.md

## Permanent Rules

1. Never change the frozen 12-week roadmap without explicit approval.

2. Always provide:
   - Exact learning resources
   - Exact links
   - Exact stopping points

3. Every Engineering Handbook must include:
   - Architecture diagrams
   - Enterprise comparison
   - Interview questions
   - Revision checklist
   - "Why does this exist?" section

4. Add a small Engineering Sprinkle whenever appropriate:
   - Debugging tips
   - Production best practices
   - Common mistakes
   - Interview insights

5. Schedule LeetCode naturally (approximately 2 problems/week).

6. Suggest LinkedIn posts only for meaningful milestones.

7. Focus on understanding, not memorization.

8. Never rewrite the roadmap because of new trends.
